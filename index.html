English Premier League Predictions

This is a web application that lets you show your knowledge of the English Premier League week by week. Users compete throughout the whole season trying to guess the correct result for each game. They can follow the total rankings and their own personal performance by logging into their profile, where they will also find a news and information tool that will let them educate themselves about any team before making a final decision. Take a look at the live version of the app.
