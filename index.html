English Premier League Predictions

github page

This is a web application that lets you show your knowledge of the English Premier League week by week. Users compete throughout the whole season trying to guess the correct result for each game. They can follow the total rankings and their own personal performance by logging into their profile, where they will also find a news and information tool that will let them educate themselves about any team before making a final decision. Take a look at the (old) but live version of the app.

Update

The link above is to the first iteration of the site and might or might not work. This project has been changing through the seasons as we learn what users prefer. I got rid of the news section and re built the whole thing with React and Node. See the new version here (it might take some time to load).

Sunday League MGMT

github page

I made this website to solve a personal problem I had with the soccer team I play with on Sundays. We never know who is showing up or not to the game and on gameday, we find ourselves scrambling to decide on a lineup. We've tried different apps to solve this problem but they are all either too complex for what we need or not meet our needs exactly. This website enables users to create a team, invite their friends to join, schedule events, confirm attendance to games, make a lineup for the next game, and notify people via text messages on when the next game will be. Take a look at the live version Here
